What is Yoga

Yoga is a mind and body practice. Various styles of yoga combine physical postures,
breathing techniques, and meditation or relaxation. Yoga is an ancient practice
that may have originated in India. It involves movement, meditation, and breathing
techniques to promote mental and physical well-being

Benefits of Yoga

Yoga's incorporation of meditation and breathing can help improve a person's
mental well-being. “Regular yoga practice creates mental clarity and calmness; increases body
awareness; relieves chronic stress patterns; relaxes the mind; centers attention;
and sharpens concentration,” says Dr. Nevins.
